Delete Your GeoFriends Account

How to Request Account Deletion

To delete your account and associated data:

  1. Send an email from the email address you used to sign up
  2. Send to: hinxlage.solutions@gmail.com
  3. Subject line: "Delete My Account"

Important: For security reasons, we can only process deletion requests sent from the email address associated with your account.

What Gets Deleted

When you request account deletion, we will permanently delete:

  • Your profile information
  • Your messages and chat history
  • Your location data
  • Your account credentials

Account deletion is processed within 30 days of your request.
